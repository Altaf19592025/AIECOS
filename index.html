Step-by-Step Recruitment Process

Transparent, merit-based selection with clear timelines and requirements.

Important Dates

Notification Date:

June 13, 2025

Application Period:

June 15 - July 15, 2025

Fee Submission Deadline:

July 17, 2025

Exam Date:

Within 15 days of application closing

1

Application Submission

Complete the online application form with all required details and documents:

  • Scanned photograph and signature
  • Educational certificates (Class 10 onwards)
  • Identity proof (Aadhaar/PAN/Passport)
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)
2

Written Examination

Fee: ₹500 (online payment only)

Format: 90 minutes, 50 multiple-choice questions

  • Numerical Ability (Class 10 level) - 15 questions
  • Reasoning Ability (Class 10 level) - 15 questions
  • English Comprehension (Class 10 level) - 20 questions

Passing Criteria: Minimum 50% marks

Refund Policy:

  • If not qualified: ₹300 refunded to source account within 7 days
  • If selected: No refund (fees applied to processing)
3

Virtual Interview

Conducted via secure video platform for written test qualifiers only.

  • Duration: 15-20 minutes
  • Technical requirements: Webcam, microphone, stable internet
  • Scheduled within 2 weeks of result declaration
  • Documents verification during interview
4

Final Placement

Based on combined merit and state-specific requirements.

Terms & Conditions:

  • No bond required for engagement
  • Early resignation requires 1 month notice to both department and company
  • PF and ESIC contributions deducted from CTC
  • Medical insurance fully covered by company
  • Initial 5-year contract, extendable based on requirements

Statewise allocation based on applicant's home state and merit ranking.

5

Result Timeline

  • Written test results: Within 10 days of exam
  • Final merit list: Within 15 days of last interview
  • Offer letters: Sent within 7 days of final list publication
  • Joining date: Typically 30 days after offer acceptance
